    System Identification is one of three critical problems in control theory:
    1. Control: Knowing G(s) and Y(t), design inputs u(t)
    2. System ID: Knowing u(t) and y(t), figure out what G(s) is
    3. Simulation: Given G(s) and u(t), identify output y(t)
    There are different ways to approach SID, from dynamics(white box), from data(black box), and hybrids(gray box)
